Optimal Tactic Engine Features

01 Synergy Selector

Analyzes current teammate selections and maps to recommend the remaining agent that historically yields the highest team win rate on that specific map geometry.

02 Economy Optimizer

Evaluates round types (eco, force, full buy) and calculates purchase recommendations to maintain a positive financial state for subsequent rounds.

03 Site Strategist

Correlates map sites against loadout ranges (e.g. recommending long-range engagements on A-site Breeze if enemy economy is predicted to be on pistols).
